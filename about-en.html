About XOGA

We are the XOGA app

The app created so organizing games is no longer a problem.

XOGA comes from a simple idea: make it easy for people to meet up and play without hassle, without arguments, and without wasting time organizing.

Our story

XOGA was born from something we all experienced: endless WhatsApp groups, people who cancel at the last minute, and games that never fill up.

We wanted a simple way to organize games, find people at our level, and focus only on playing. That is how XOGA started: an app made by and for players.

What drives us

Mission

Make it easy for anyone to play their favorite sport, whenever and with whoever they want.

Vision

Create an active, connected and accessible sports community for everyone.

Our values

Simplicity

Less organizing, more playing.

Community

Playing is better when shared.

Real sport

Built for people, not for statistics.

Commitment

Respect for everyone's time and effort.

Who XOGA is for

For people who play after work.

For those who want to compete without pressure.

For anyone who just wants the game to happen.
