Cookie policy

This Cookie Policy applies to the use of the XOGA application (the "App"), accessible from mobile devices. This document is available on the XOGA website so users can consult it.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on a user's device when they use an application or website. In a mobile app context, cookies and similar technologies enable proper service operation and session management.

Technologies used in the App

The XOGA App uses technologies equivalent to cookies, such as session identifiers, local storage, or tokens, necessary to:

  • Allow the user to sign in and stay signed in
  • Ensure account security
  • Enable proper app operation
  • Improve the user experience

Types of cookies and technologies used

Technical and necessary technologies

The App uses strictly necessary technologies to:

  • Authenticate the user
  • Keep the session active
  • Manage internal navigation
  • Ensure service security

These technologies do not require prior consent because they are essential for the App to work.

Third-party technologies

Some third-party technical services (for example, hosting, databases, or notifications) may use technical identifiers needed to provide the service.

XOGA does not use advertising or tracking cookies for commercial purposes.

Managing cookies and similar technologies

Since the App uses only necessary technical technologies, it is not possible to disable them without affecting the service.

The user can delete the App at any time from their mobile device, which will automatically remove associated browsing data.

Policy updates

XOGA reserves the right to modify this Cookie Policy to adapt it to technical or legal changes. Any changes will be published on the XOGA website.
