FAQ

Answers to the most common questions about XOGA

What is XOGA?

XOGA is an app that lets you create and join sports games easily, organizing times, spots and levels without hassle.

Is XOGA free?

Yes, XOGA is free. Advanced features may be added in the future, but playing and organizing games is completely free.

Which sports can I find?

Right now you can create and join soccer, basketball, tennis and volleyball games. We are working to add more sports.

Do I need to create a game to play?

No. You can join games created by other users near your location and matching your level.

How does player level work?

Level is defined based on your profile information and feedback from other players to keep games balanced.

What happens if someone does not show up?

XOGA lets you confirm attendance and manage spots to reduce no-shows and improve organization.

Can I cancel my attendance?

Yes, you can cancel before the game starts so another player can take your spot.

Is XOGA available across Spain?

Yes, you can use XOGA in any city. Game availability depends on activity in your area.

Do I need an account to use XOGA?

Yes, you need an account to participate in games and keep the experience safe for everyone.

Is my data protected?

Yes. Data security and privacy are a priority. We only use data to improve your experience in the app.

Can’t find what you are looking for?

Write to us and we will help you as soon as possible.
